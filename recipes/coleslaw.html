Crunchy Coleslaw

Light and Crunchy

Quick and easy coleslaw recipe, can be prepared in advance. Perfect for Summer BBQs, pair with cooked meat for a sure-fire hit.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 white cabbage
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 celery stick
  • 1 red-skinned apple
  • 1 carrot, large
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard

Steps

  1. Slice the cabbage thinly and add to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Peel the onion and finely chop, thinly slice the celery and add both.
  3. Peel and then grate the carrot and add to the bowl.
  4. Grate the apple with the skin on, leaving only the core and add to the bowl and mix everything together.
  5. Into the bowl, add the mayonnaise, yoghurt and mustard. Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix well.
