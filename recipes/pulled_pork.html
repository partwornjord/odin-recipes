Pulled Pork

Slow cooked & Delicious

A simple, delicious Pulled Pork recipe. Minimal prep time and cooked slow over an afternoon, the result will melt in the mouth however you choose to serve it.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups of cider
  • Pork shoulder (boneless) ~2.5kg
  • Coleslaw of your choice
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Mix together the smoked paprika, cumin, pepper, brown sugar and salt. Rub the mixture over the pork shoulder.
  2. Place the pork in a large casserole dish, skin side up and pour in the cider.
  3. Pre-heat the oven to 150C (130C fan). Cook pork for 4-8 hours, or until it begins to fall apart, checking ever hour to check. If the meet begins to look dry, add more cider as appropriate.
  4. Remove dish from the oven. Place the meat in a clean dish leaving the liquid in the casserole dish.
  5. Remove the top layer of skin and fat. Using 2 forks, shred the meat.
  6. Add BBQ sauce to taste and mix well.
